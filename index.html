About_

Greetings! I'm Gareth, a freshly minted software developer hailing from the vibrant tech scene, having just graduated from the esteemed Northcoders bootcamp. My journey to the world of coding is a tale of transformation, originating from a rich background in domestic construction. For two decades, I specialized in crafting high-end bathrooms, kitchens, and mastering the art of tiling, flooring, and joinery. However, my insatiable thirst for new challenges led me on a two-year exploration to discover my true calling. This quest culminated in a life-altering decision to delve into the dynamic realm of software development. Northcoders became my guiding light, providing a rigorous and immersive learning experience that equipped me with the skills to embark on a fulfilling career in tech. Now, armed with a blend of craftsmanship from construction and the latest coding techniques, I'm on the lookout for my inaugural role as a software developer. Beyond the world of coding, you'll find me reveling in the crisp air of winter walks, exploring hiking trails, and carving paths down ski slopes. Two furry companions accompany me on these adventures, ensuring there's never a dull moment. In 2018, I achieved a personal triumph by summiting Mont Blanc, a metaphorical ascent mirroring my journey from construction to coding. Eager to contribute my unique background and freshly acquired coding skills, I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in the tech landscape. Let's connect and explore how my diverse experiences can bring innovation and a fresh perspective to your team!
